The problem

SMBs are stuck between Tally and chaos.

India's SMB market is massive, underserved and uniquely complicated — GST across multiple GSTIN entities, India-specific banking integrations, mixed cash-and-digital transactions and a CA ecosystem that still runs on spreadsheets.

Digital Muneem automates the ledger work: bank feeds are auto-imported, transactions are AI-categorised, GST returns are pre-computed and the CA portal integration makes filing a two-click job.

Feature · Core

AI Transaction Tagging

Every bank entry is automatically categorised using a fine-tuned classifier trained on Indian SMB transaction patterns. Override in one click.

Feature · Compliance

GST-Aware Ledger

Multi-GSTIN support, automatic GSTR-1/3B computation, e-invoice generation and reconciliation against GSTN portal data.

Feature · Multi-entity

Multi-Entity Management

Manage multiple firms under one login. Consolidated P&L across entities, inter-company eliminations and per-entity tax reporting.

Feature · Ops

Feature · Reports

Daily close automatically. P&L, balance sheet and cash-flow statement always current — no month-end crunch.
