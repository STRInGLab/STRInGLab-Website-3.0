Why blockchain

Swift is slow. Correspondent banking is expensive. There's a better way.

Traditional cross-border transfers lose 3–7% in fees and take 1–5 business days to settle. Both problems are structural — each correspondent bank takes a cut and holds float. Remit removes the intermediaries.

On-chain settlement happens in minutes. Smart contracts enforce compliance rules — KYC, AML, sanctions screening — automatically, with an immutable audit trail that satisfies regulators without the manual overhead of traditional compliance workflows.
