Project Detail:

A holistic online platform designed to assist prospective students in making informed decisions about higher education by providing detailed information about colleges, courses, exams, and more. The platform offers insights, reviews, and application support for multiple educational institutions.

Problem Statement:

With the vast number of educational institutions and programs available, prospective students often face challenges in accessing accurate and comprehensive information. They need a reliable platform that not only offers detailed educational data but also aids in the decision-making process.

Solution:

Developed a centralized online portal that aggregates data from various educational institutions. Incorporated user reviews, course details, admission processes, and examination schedules to provide an end-to-end solution for students seeking higher education insights.

Features:

  • Comprehensive College Listings: Detailed profiles of institutions with courses, fees, placement, ranking, and more.
  • Course Finder: Enables students to find courses based on interest, location, and budget.
  • Reviews and Ratings: Authentic user reviews to assist in decision-making.
  • Examination Calendar: Schedule of upcoming entrance exams with eligibility criteria and application details.
  • News: Trending news and articles related to colleges globally.
  • Events: Post and Register for events happening Physically, Online or recordings.

Use Cases:

  • A student seeking information about top engineering colleges in a specific region.
  • An individual looking to understand the admission process for a particular course.
  • A parent wanting to read reviews and ratings of an institution before making a decision for their child.

Web/Application Specific Points:

  • User Interface (UI): A clean, intuitive design with easy navigation. Dynamic filters allow users to narrow down their search based on various parameters.
  • User Experience (UX): Designed with the user in mind, the platform offers personalized recommendations, seamless navigation, and mobile responsiveness.
  • Interactivity: Users can save their favorite colleges, set reminders for application deadlines, interact with graphs/charts for fee structures, and participate in community forums.

Technologies Used:

  • Backend: Fast API, Python
  • Frontend: React.js, Bootstrap, React Native
  • Database: PostgreSQL
  • Cloud: Digital Ocean
