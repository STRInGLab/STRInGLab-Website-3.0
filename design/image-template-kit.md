## Master templates to create in Figma

1. **Insight tile** - paper background, mono category at top, 5-9 word serif claim, one acid-green signal/rule, source at bottom.
2. **Case-study metric** - ink background, huge outcome number, one line of context, supporting system image in the final third.
3. **Service explainer** - 3-step system path with one selected green node; no more than 35 words.
4. **Product proof** - cropped UI placed on paper, a single product benefit and subtle metadata.
5. **Quote / point of view** - ink field, short serif quote, speaker/role in mono, no quotation-mark decoration.
6. **Event / announcement** - paper field, bold date in mono, one action-oriented headline, logo lockup in footer.

For all templates, create desktop and mobile text variants. Keep the logo at its native aspect ratio, with a clear space equal to the height of the first capital letter in the wordmark.

## Image-generation prompts

All prompts assume: `editorial technology image, premium consulting brand, deep ink #0B0B0C, warm paper #F2EEE6, restrained acid green #C6FF4A, no readable text, no logo, no watermark, no stock-photo look`.

### AI and machine learning

`A precise production AI system map: model evaluation traces, retrieval paths, confidence bands and monitored outputs flowing across a dark ink canvas; a single acid-green route marks the chosen response; warm paper annotation cards, crisp technical editorial photography aesthetic, ample negative space on the left for copy, 16:9.`

### Data analytics

`An elegant business intelligence scene built from real-looking dashboard fragments: cohort curve, revenue bridge, supply chain exceptions and data-quality status; dark ink background, paper chart cards, one acid-green decisive trend, legible structure but no readable UI copy, top-left negative space, 16:9.`

### Workflow automation

`A workflow orchestration canvas connecting CRM, ERP, document extraction and API services; clean event paths and exception branch, selected automated route in acid green, warm paper nodes on black ink, rigorous editorial system diagram, no floating icons, 16:9.`

### Operations research

`A supply-chain optimization map with warehouse nodes, vehicle routes, constraint boundaries and one highlighted optimal path; technical yet beautifully restrained, black ink terrain, paper map layers, acid-green route only, premium analytical editorial design, 16:9.`

### Product engineering

`A refined arrangement of responsive enterprise product interfaces showing web dashboard, mobile workflow and design-system details; tactile paper background meeting deep black device frames, one acid-green active state, no people, no generic gradients, 16:9.`

### Document intelligence

`An enterprise document AI review workspace with invoice scan, clean OCR bounding boxes, extracted fields, confidence indicators and human-review decision point; warm paper documents against dark ink UI, acid green only for validated fields, editorial precision, 16:9.`

## Artworking checklist

- Does the image communicate an actual system or operational result?
- Is green restricted to the single thing that matters?
- Is there a clear low-detail zone for crop and overlay copy?
- Is the image still readable as a small social thumbnail?
- Are any diagrams internally coherent, free of fake UI text and free of invented logos?
