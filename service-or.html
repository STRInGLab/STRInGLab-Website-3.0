Operations research

When Excel stops working, the maths begins.

Operations research gives you rigorous, mathematically optimal — or near-optimal — answers to problems that are too complex for intuition. Routing 200 trucks. Scheduling 400 staff shifts. Sourcing across 50 suppliers with fluctuating prices and lead times.

We combine OR with simulation so you can test optimized policies against realistic uncertainty before committing. Then we wrap the model in a clear UI your ops team can run without a data scientist in the room.
