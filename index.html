§ Process · 05 phases

How the work actually moves.

No mystery. Same five phases, every engagement — discovery, design, build, deploy, scale. We tell you what week you're in, what we're shipping, and what blocked us.

Phase 01 · 1 wk

Discover.

Workshop your goals, constraints and data. Leave with a signed brief and a north-star metric.

  • Stakeholder interviews
  • Data & system audit
  • Success metrics
Phase 02 · 2 wks

Design.

Architect the system end-to-end — flows, data model, model cards, UX — before we write production code.

  • Tech architecture
  • UI & flow prototypes
  • Model design docs
Phase 03 · 4–12 wks

Build.

Weekly ship cadence. Loom-first updates, PR-level visibility, and a working preview from week one.

  • Weekly production ships
  • Staging + test coverage
  • Human-in-the-loop QA
Phase 04 · 1 wk

Deploy.

Production cutover with full runbooks, monitoring, and a rollback plan that actually works.

  • Runbooks & alerts
  • Team training
  • Live rollback drill
Phase 05 · ongoing

Scale.

Retainer support, model re-training, and a roadmap that keeps compounding long after launch day.

  • Quarterly reviews
  • Model drift monitoring
  • Feature roadmap

§ Frequently Asked

Questions, answered plainly.

Don't see yours? Send a note at info@stringlab.org — we reply within a business day.

How soon can we get started?+
Most discovery workshops kick off within a week. A signed brief and production-shape delivery plan usually lands by end of week two.
What's a typical engagement size?+
Anywhere from a 2-week data audit to a 6-month product build. Retainers start at a fractional data-science seat; full product pods scale from there.
Do you work with early-stage startups?+
Yes — we run a lightweight engagement track for pre-seed and seed teams, with equity-friendly pricing and weekly strategic check-ins.
Who owns the IP we build together?+
You do, always. Standard contracts assign all code, models, and artifacts to the client on delivery. Our internal tooling stays ours.
How do you handle data security & compliance?+
We run SOC2-track practices — encryption at rest and in transit, least-privilege access, signed DPAs, and a named security owner on every engagement.
Can you take over an existing codebase?+
Yes. We do takeover engagements regularly — first week is a code & infra audit, then a remediation plan with a fixed-price first milestone.
